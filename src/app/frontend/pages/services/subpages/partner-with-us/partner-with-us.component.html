Using Startup Designs as a service provider

White label

White labelling to Startup Designs allows your company to offer all of Startup Designs's services without the added responsibility of delivering them yourself. We work in the background directly with your team and remain anonymous to your clients. We are transparent with our cost estimations which allows you decide your own pricing structure and deal directly with your clients.

Referrals

Depending on your capacity you might not have the time or want the hassles that come with managing a web development project. Referrals allow us to compliment your services and work directly with your client.

You don't need to have any web design & development experience for referring clients to us and we offer a commission for every successful project that you referr to us.
